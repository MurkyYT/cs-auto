Features of CSAuto

Auto accept matches

CSAuto can automatically accept matches for you,

All you have to do is wait for the match to be found,

You can go do some stuff while the match is being searched

Auto buy armor and defuse kit

Automatically buys defuse kit for you as a ct,

Automatically buys armor and auto refills it when it is 70 or bellow,

Closes chat and console when buying and only buys at freezetime

Auto pause or play spotify song

Automatically pauses spotify song while you are alive,

When you die resumes the song,

That way you can listen to a song and still hear the game when needed

Auto reload weapon

Automatically reloads your weapon when you have 0 bullets left,

Can continue spraying afterwards, sometimes it is lagging out



Discord Rich Presence

Shows when you are in lobby your friendcode and how long you have been there

When in game shows current map, match score, your team, current round state, and the game mode
